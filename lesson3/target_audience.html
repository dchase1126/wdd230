Target Audience

Chamber offers a variety of programs and services for you to learn, grow and meet other businesses and community leaders in the greater Idaho Falls area. Take your business to the next level with our programs and services. As a Chamber member, you become part of a group of companies and organizations that invest in the greater Idaho Falls area, it's growth and prosperity.

Perona 1

Stephanie Lindquist

Stephanie a natural light photographer who loves to help capture sweet memory moments that her customers can treasure for years to come. Nothing brings her more joy then to look back at past photos and relive them. Her biggest passion and desire is for customers that come to her, will love having their pictures taken by her. Stephanie's goal is to help peopl in the Idaho Falls community capture their memories with quality images. She doesn't want her customers to regret not photographing where they are in life. She want to preserve that for them. It is important for her to provide this service without breaking the bank for her customers, so she is wanting to learn from other business owners by joining the Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce.
