Typography

The Typography that will be used is Cookie and Sansita fonts.

Cookie

The Cookie font is a script typeface based on brush calligraphy. It has a little bit of 1950s style that makes you think about all the beautiful ads and pin-ups from this time. It is sweet and friendly - but not too decorative; simple and legible even in text sizes.

Sansita

Sansita is a tasty new Omnibus type font designed by Pablo Cosgaya. The flavor of Sansita's lowercase explores the relationship between typography and calligraphy. Sansita is a tasty typeface family, with 6 weight variants, plus matching italics. Each stroke weight has its own allure.
