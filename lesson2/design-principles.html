Hick's Law

Disney Plus
disneyplus.com

This is an example of Hick's law. Disneyplus has put many choices of movies on their website. By doing this, it will take the veiwer a longer time to decide what they want to see and the veiwer will stay on their website longer.

Contrast

Best Buy
bestbuy.com

This is an example of Contrast.
Contrasting colors of Blue, Yellow, Red, and White is appealing to the eyes of the veiwer making it easy to see the pictures and the text with better clarity on the page.

White Space

BYU-Pathway Worldwide
byupathway.org

This is an example of White space. The clean crisp space between the focal points of the webpage. The crips line that is created with the yellow of the shirt against the white back ground is pleasing to the veiwer. The white space between the text and the image creates white space.
